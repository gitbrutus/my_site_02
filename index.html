The one-sheeter hub

Despite various internal platforms to find core search product information, it is oftentimes cumbersome and time-consuming to dig through various links and sub-sites to find, filter and compare relevant information.

Managed and maintained by the CE Core Search team, the one-sheeter hub lists only high quality, pre-selected documents, allowing Googlers to find and share relevant information without the need to tediously search, filter, cross-check and edit contents.

The one-sheeters (documents)

Many existing one-sheeters, broadly speaking, lack proper contents quality assurance (e.g. product lead/specialist review), are one-off creations missing important updates (e.g. performance metrics), or simply lack focus to providing relevant, concise, and consistent visually appealing information - thus resulting in a suboptimal user experience.

All one-sheeters listed on this hub contain concise and externally friendly information comparable to 3 to 4 full-size google docs/slides (font size 10), go through detailed quality assurance, and keep focus on relevant contents by including additional deep dive links to Adwords help center and/or case studies.
