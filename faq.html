Google Core Search

One-Sheeter Hub

Concise and up-to-date one-sheeters across core search products - including Automation, Ads, and Audiences - for pitching, referencing, and training.
Quality Assurance

Each one-sheeters has been created and reviewed by at least three search specialists and/or product leads.

Focus & Relevance

Covering most relevant product questions: what it is - how it works - how it performs - why to use it. No fluff.

Externally Save

All contents - including case studies and performance metrics - can be shared directly with clients.

FAQ

Answers to your most frequent questions

Can I share the one-sheeters externally?

Yes, both as PDF or direct link.

Can I forward my clients to the website directly?

No, the site is internal only and not accessible without linked google.com account.

Are the sheeters available in Google Slides/Docs (to edit)?

No. Sheeters are created in Adobe InDesign and are only available as PDF.

Errors? Bug? Feedback?

Please send an email to ce-search-specialists@google.com. Do preferably not contact the product leads/ sheet authors directly concerning bugs.

(When) will you make a one-sheeter for product ‘xy’?

Although there is currently no particular (public) pipeline, we are happy to hear suggestions which products require a (new) one-sheeter. Note that this site only covers core search products.

When/how often are the sheeters updated?

Sheeters are updated regularly based on major product changes, most recently July 2018 (see footer of the cards on the main page). However, if in doubt, refer to the particular product comm-doc to cross check.

Question regarding contents of a particular one-sheeter - who to ask?

Generic product questions are not the scope of this one-sheeter hub. Any particular questions about the very content of a one-sheeter can be addressed to the authors of each product sheeter (names listed in the footer of the cards).

Why can’t I download/share the one-sheeters (e.g. link does not work)?

Ensure you are logged in with your google.com email address, and open the website with Google chrome (not internet explorer or safari). If the problem persists, please send an email to ce-search-specialists@google.com.

Who is in charge of this site/project?

This site/project is managed by the CE Search Specialist team.
