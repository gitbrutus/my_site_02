The one-sheeter hub

The one-sheeter hub is a collection of core search product one-sheeters, managed and maintained by the Central Europe Core Search team.

Although there are various internal platforms to find core search product information, it is oftentimes cumbersome and time-consuming to dig through various links and sub-sites to find, filter and compare relevant information about core search products.

The one-sheeter hub lists only high quality, pre-selected and externally friendly documents, allowing Googlers to find and share relevant information without the need to tediously search, filter, cross-check and edit contents.

The one-sheeters (documents)

Many existing one-sheeters, broadly speaking, lack proper contents quality review by product lead/specialist, are one-off creations missing regular product updates, or simply lack consistency in providing relevant, concise, and visually appealing information design - resulting in a suboptimal user experience.

All one-sheeters listed on this hub go through detailed quality review, contain concise and externally friendly information - comparable to 3 to 4 full-size google docs/slides - and keep focus on relevant contents by including additional deep dive links to the Google Ads help center and/or case studies.
